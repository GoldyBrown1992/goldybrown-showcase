GOLDY BROWN

Closing the Show

As the finale designer, Goldy Brown brings the perfect fusion of luxury and street culture to Vancouver's fashion scene. Known for pushing boundaries with bold designs that celebrate multicultural identity and modern elegance, Goldy Brown's collection promises to be the unforgettable climax of the evening.

This exclusive showcase features pieces that redefine contemporary fashion—where high-end craftsmanship meets urban authenticity. Each garment tells a story of innovation, cultural pride, and fearless self-expression.
